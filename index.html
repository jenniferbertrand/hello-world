Current Postdoctoral Work

I am currently a postdoctoral fellow, conducting research within the Department of Medicine at the University of Alberta. My work explores how digital health innovations, such as advanced motion capture technologies + wearables, can support and enhance physical assessments in telehealth settings. This research is designed to make assessments more accessible, particularly for individuals managing chronic conditions, improving both patient care and clinical outcomes.
